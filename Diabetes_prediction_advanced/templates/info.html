What is diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic condition where your body has difficulty regulating blood glucose (sugar). Over time, elevated glucose can affect the heart, blood vessels, nerves, eyes, and kidneys. Managing risk early helps prevent complications.

Common symptoms

  • Increased thirst and frequent urination
  • Unexplained weight changes and fatigue
  • Blurred vision or slow-healing wounds

Risk factors

  • Family history of diabetes
  • Overweight or obesity, limited physical activity
  • High blood pressure or cholesterol levels

Prevention & lifestyle

  • Balanced diet: whole grains, vegetables, lean proteins
  • Regular activity: at least 150 minutes/week moderate exercise
  • Sleep well and manage stress
